Your Journey Starts Here

SINGAPORE, an awesome tourist destination, is famous for being a global financial center, being among the most densely populated places in the world, having a world-class city airport with a waterfall, and a Botanic Garden that is a World Heritage , breathtaking views and much more to experience.

GARDENS BY THE BAY:- The Supertree Grove is home to Gardens by the Bay's most iconic structures and a great location for unforgettable vistas. Soaring, surreal architecture, diverse gardens and pristine groves truly make it a sight to behold

MERLION PARK:- Merlion Park is a famous Singapore landmark and a major tourist attraction. The Merlion is the official mascot of Singapore. This Half-lion, Half-fish icon is a mythical creature that symbolizes the city-state and its roots. attraction. It is depicted as a mythical creature with the head of a lion and the body of a fish.

CHINATOWN:- For a fascinating peek into Singapore’s Chinese culture and history, Chinatown is good place to start. Here, you’ll enjoy a mix of heritage visits to museums, shopping as well as a good variety of food options, sure to leave a traveller happy and satisfied at the end of the day.

SINGAPORE FLYER:- Located in the heart of downtown Marina Bay, the Singapore Flyer is Asia's largest giant observation wheel. More importantly, it is high on thrills for tourists and locals alike. Step into one of the 28 fully air-conditioned glass capsules, and be transported on a 30-minute journey of stunning day and night scenes.

SAND SKYPARK OBSERVATION DECK:- On the roof of Marina Bay Sands, the Sands SkyPark Observation Deck boasts scenic views of the panoramic vistas of Marina Bay and Singapore’s world-class cityscape.

MONTEVERDE CLOUD FOREST:- Cloud Forests are unique ecosystems, they only represent less than 1% of the remaining forests on the planet's surface. Here you can find very particular species of trees, plants and animals, such as the numerous species of orchids.

MARINA BAY:- Besides housing and the most fantastically designed buildings. Marina Bay is one of the tourist attractions buzzing art scene, options for dining, shopping & entertainment, water sports, and river cruises.
