MALDIVES, a famous destination for tourist, The Maldives is famous for a number of reasons, including its vibrant culture, pristine beaches, sprawling greenery, and fascinating landmarks. Apart from the numerous enchanting landmarks, Maldives is also famous for water sports and other adventure activities. that you would love to experience.

MAAFUSHI:- If you have only a couple of days to enjoy Maldives, Maafushi is a great local island option because they have better infrastructure and it is near the airport. Easy to access: Maafushi to become a popular destination for tourists visiting local islands.

CONRAD MALDIVES RANGALI ISLAND:- Escape to Rangali Island, an untouched slice of paradise. Revel in our pools, with mesmerizing Indian Ocean views. Find bliss in your island sanctuary. Discover private gardens, plunge-pools, and stairs leading into the Indian Ocean.

ALIMATHAA:- :-Alimathaa Island has white-sand beaches and clear waters that tempt tourists from all over the world. If you are interested in exploring marine life comprising colourful coral reefs and a wide variety of fishes (eels, jack fish, and moray), then this destination in the Maldives is an ideal choice

NATIONAL MUSEUM:- The National Museum of Maldives is an ancient building containing a well-maintained collection of artefact that help in tracing unusual historical events.

COMO COCOA ISLAND:- Stay on Our Private Island. White Sand Shores. Sandbanks that Stretch. Encircled by Coral. Located a Short 40 Min Speedboat Ride from the Airport. Ideal for Quick Maldives Escape.
