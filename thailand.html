THAILAND, With epic tropical beauty, ancient temples, amazing food scene and vibrant nightlife, Thailand truly has a chaotic charm to it that attracts people from across the globe. The country, also referred to as the Land of Smiles, never disappoints. National Park is home to protected habitats for the greatest number of animal species in Thailand.

THE GRAND PLACE:- Located in the heart of Bangkok, the Grand Palace is in fact a complex of buildings and has been the King of Siam's official residence since it was built in 1782. It's also been the home of the Thai King, Royal Court and the administrative seat of government for more than 150 years. As a tourist this place is worth visiting.

KHAO YAI NATIONAL PARK:- Khao Yai National Park is home to protected habitats for the greatest number of animal species in Thailand. Its food sources attract many charismatic species which can often be viewed by visitors: wild elephant, gaur, dhole, muntjac (barking deer), sambar deer, gibbon, hornbill and macaque.The top attractions to visit in Khao Yai National Park are:

  • Haew Narok Waterfall
  • Primo Piazza Khao Yai
  • Khao Luk Chang Bat Cave
  • Night Safari Korat
  • The Bloom By TV Pool
