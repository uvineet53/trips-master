Malaysia, Malaysia is south east Asian country occupying parts of the Malay peninsula and the island of Borneo . It’s known for its beaches, rainforests and mix of Malay , Chinese, Indian and Europe cultural influences. The capital , Kuala Lumpur, is home to colonial buildings, busy shopping districts, skyscrapers such as the iconic Petronas Twin towers.

THE PETRONAS TOWERS:-A pair of super tall skyscrapers also known as twin towers. It has awesome view over Kuala lumpur city.

BATU CAVES:- One of the countries biggest attractions and a place of pilgrimage for Tamil Hindus.That has series of caves and cave temples in Malaysia.

TAMAN NEGARA:- Here you should Visit for its unmatched flora and fauna or to get the hang of the original inhabitants of Malaysia, the Orang Asli tribe. You get to walk on the longest canopy walkway, experience trekking under the rainforest canopy, fishing, camping, observing wildlife, and so much more.

KL BIRD PARK:- :- Major attractions of the Kuala Lumpur Bird Park are Hornbill Park, parrots, the egg incubation area, flamingo pond, bird gallery, and others. A visit to the KL Bird Park is a must if one wants to enjoy the beauty of nature with colorful birds around them.

AQUARIA KLCC:- Aquaria KLCC houses close to 5000 species of marine and terrestrial animals. They have different packages that will help you interact intimately with the animals. They have an underwater tunnel, Living Ocean, which houses five species of sharks and turtles! That attracts tourists.

BAKO NATIONAL PARK:- With its rainforest, abundant wildlife, jungle streams, waterfalls, unique flora and fauna, secluded beaches, and trekking trails, Bako National Park offers visitors an excellent introduction to the rainforest of Borneo.

LANGKAWI SKY BRIDGE:- Witness panoramic views due to the beautiful curved architecture of the bridge that lets you catch every detail. Experience the rush of adrenaline by standing on the transparent floor of the bridge to experience hauntingly beautiful heights as you look beneath.
