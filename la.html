LOS ANGELES, is the entertainment capital of the world, offering myriad attractions and activities: movie studios, the Sunset Strip, Venice Beach, landmark museums, world-famous theme parks, wineries, breweries, outdoor activities, exciting nightlife, and much more, to visit.

LOS ANGELES COUNTY MUSEUM OF ARTS:- Art museum with the indoor and outdoor exhibits (including the LA Lights and Levitating Mass exhibits). It's a good spot to bring first time visitors LACMA houses more than 120,000 objects across different media, region and periods such as Greek, Roman and Etruscan art, Asian art, American and Latin American art, decorative arts and design, photography, and modern and contemporary art.

THE GETTY CENTER:- The J. Paul Getty Museum at the Getty Center in Los Angeles houses European paintings, drawings, sculpture, illuminated manuscripts, decorative arts, and photography from its beginnings to the present, gathered internationally. The Getty Center, in Los Angeles, California, is a campus of the Getty Museum and other programs of the Getty Trust. The $1.3 billion center opened to the public, is well known for its architecture, gardens, and views overlooking Los Angeles.

UNIVERSAL STUDIOS HOLLYWOOD WALT DISNEY CONCERT HALL:- Home to LA Phil and the Los Angeles Master Chorale, Walt Disney Concert Hall is one of the most acoustically sophisticated concert halls in the world and provides both visual and aural intimacy through its exterior and interior design. For tourists its worth visiting.

DISNEYLAND PARK:- It is home to iconic rides such as Pirates of the Caribbean, The Haunted Mansion, Matterhorn Bobsleds and It's a Small World. It's also the only Disney Park to feature New Orleans Square - one of Walt's favorite lands.
